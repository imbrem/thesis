# Paper Formalizations (Reference Only)

These are the Lean 4 formalizations developed alongside the papers. They are included as **git submodules for reference only** and are **not built in CI**.

- **discretion/** — github.com/imbrem/discretion (Lean 4 v4.20.0-rc5)
- **debruijn-ssa/** — github.com/imbrem/debruijn-ssa (Lean 4 v4.15.0-rc1)

## Status

These formalizations were developed against older Lean 4 toolchains and mathlib versions. They may no longer compile with current toolchains without updating their dependencies.

## Future work

- Update these projects to compile with a current Lean 4 toolchain and mathlib
- Summarize the key definitions, theorems, and proof strategies for AI-assisted development
- Integrate relevant parts into the thesis formalization (`formalization/thesis/`)

## For AI agents

Do not attempt to build or modify these submodules. They are pinned to specific commits and should be treated as read-only references. The active formalization work happens in `formalization/thesis/`.
